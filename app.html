About the Demo

Our iOS application enables customers with PowerPath devices to detect "Nearby Devices" within the vicinity of the mobile device. Select a tag to learn details such as the location or the usage state of a particuliar asset.

To activate the demo mode, select the "Settings" button on the bottom right. On the next screen, activate "Expert Mode". Now when you go back to Nearby Devices you will see SIM in upper left corner. In the upper left corner of the application, you will see "Sim" highlighted in blue.

Select this and you will see a simulated list of items in the "Nearby Devices" menu. Scroll or select any device to see an example of the data being collected per asset or temp tag.

Installing the Emanate iOS 'PowerPath' App

1. Open the following URL (https://apps.emanatewireless.com) in the iOS mobile Safari browser app.

2. Click on the 'PowerPath' app icon to download and install the 'PowerPath' app.

3. The Emanate 'PowerPath' app will now be installed on your device.

4. Apple requires that each "enterprise" app be trusted by the user. When you first open the Emanate 'PowerPath' iOS app, you will see a notification that the developer of the app is not trusted on your device.

5. After dismissing this message you can establish trust for the "Emanate Wireless, Inc." app developer. Tap Settings > General > Device Management (or Profiles). You then see a a profile for the developer under the "Enterprise App" heading.

6. Tap the "Emanate Wireless, Inc." profile to establish trust for this developer.

7. Tap the "Trust Emanate Wireless, Inc." button to trust apps from this developer.

8. Tap the "Trust" button to trust the PowerPath app.

9. The PowerPath app can now be launched.
