Know Sooner.

PowerPath™ Temp alerts you to temperature-control problems before they happen.

Stay Chill

Designed for healthcare and pharmaceutical environments requiring temperature control, PowerPath Temp monitors internal temperature and power consumption of any device designed for temperature control.

It can detect open doors, laboring compressors, and AC power outages, alerting end users before temperatures are out of spec. Protect valuable cold-chain products and use trend data for analysis and predictive maintenance.

  • No battery maintenance
  • Provides configurable range of temperature monitoring
  • Wi-Fi enabled
  • Works with our convenient smartphone app
  • Reports available for compliance requirements
  • Quick deployment
  • Easy to use

Beyond monitoring temperature control, PowerPath Temp provides utilization reporting and raw data to help with compliance and predicitive maintenance. You’ll gain complete visibility into all of your temperature-controlled systems. Your biomedical and clinical staff will be able to access immediate information regarding all refrigerated devices, and you’ll be able to track and monitor temperature within refrigerators or freezers campus wide, locally or remotely.

Best of all, because PowerPath Temp monitors the electrical current of all temperature-control devices you’ll know when temperature control systems are failing before temperatures are out of spec. That means you’ll be able to avoid loss of valuable drugs, vaccines, human tissue, and other expensive medical resource.
